1. Scope of this policy

This policy applies only to the Stupid Simple Finance Android application and the services it directly uses. The app is a personal finance tracker built around a "local-first" principle: it is designed to work fully offline, and your data stays on your device unless you actively enable an optional cloud feature. This policy does not cover any third-party website or service that may be linked from the app.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information stored on your device (always local)

The following is created and stored entirely within the app's private storage on your device. We cannot access it, and it is never transmitted unless you enable cloud backup:

  • Financial information you enter: transactions, income, expenses, investments, accounts, budgets, goals, categories, and any notes.
  • App preferences such as theme and currency.
  • If you enable App Lock: a salted, hashed representation of your PIN. The PIN itself is never stored, and the hash cannot be used to recover it.

2.2 Information collected only if you sign in (optional)

The app offers an optional Google Sign-In feature for cloud backup. If, and only if, you choose to sign in, we collect and process:

  • Your Google account identifier, email address, and display name, via Firebase Authentication, to identify your account.
  • A copy of the financial information described in section 2.1, uploaded to a secured cloud database (Google Firebase Firestore) so it can be backed up and restored across devices. Each user's data is access-restricted to that user's own account.

2.3 Subscription information

If you purchase the optional "Safe plan" subscription, the purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. We receive confirmation of your subscription status (active or not). We do not receive or store your card number or any payment details.

2.4 Diagnostic and technical information

The app may use Firebase Crashlytics to send anonymous crash reports and basic device information (such as device model, operating system version, and app version) when the app encounters an error. This helps us identify and fix stability problems. This information is not used to personally identify you.

2.5 Notifications

With your permission, the app may show occasional reminders to back up your data. These reminders are generated locally on your device.

4. Legal bases for processing

Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or similar laws apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for optional features such as cloud sign-in, backup, and notifications. You may withdraw consent at any time by signing out or disabling the feature.
  • Performance of a contract — to deliver subscription features you have purchased.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the app stable and secure (for example, anonymous crash diagnostics), balanced against your privacy.

5. Sharing and service providers

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with the service providers ("processors") needed to operate the app, and only to the extent necessary:

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users or others.

6. International data transfers

If you use the optional cloud features, your data may be stored and processed on Google's servers, which may be located in countries other than your own. Google maintains safeguards for such transfers in line with applicable data protection laws. If you prefer your data never to leave your device, simply do not sign in.

7. Data retention

  • On-device data is retained until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app, which removes all locally stored data.
  • Cloud data (if you signed in) is retained until you request its deletion or delete your account, as described in section 9.
  • Diagnostic data is retained only as long as needed to investigate and fix issues, in line with the provider's standard retention periods.

8. How we protect your data

  • On-device data is held in the app's private storage sandbox, isolated from other apps.
  • The optional App Lock adds a PIN or biometric gate; the PIN is stored only as a salted hash.
  • All data transmitted to cloud services travels over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections.
  • Cloud data is protected by per-user access rules, so only your authenticated account can read or write your own data.
No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure. While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend keeping your device locked and your Google account secured.

9. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Export or receive a copy of your data (the app includes built-in export and backup tools).
  • Withdraw consent for optional features at any time.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.

You can exercise most of these directly in the app: export your data from Settings, sign out to stop cloud syncing, or uninstall to remove all local data. For requests we need to handle for you, contact us using the details in section 12. We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

10. Deleting your data

  • Local data: delete records within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all on-device data.
  • Cloud data: sign out at any time to stop further syncing. To permanently delete the data associated with your account from our cloud database, email us at the address below and we will delete it. We aim to action verified requests within 30 days.

11. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be highlighted within the app. Your continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
